Effective Date: January 1, 2026

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your TangoDating account at any time from within the app:

  1. Open Settings
  2. Tap Delete Account
  3. Confirm deletion

If you cannot access your account, you may request account deletion by emailing us at mttotango@hotmail.com.

What Data Is Deleted

When you delete your account, we delete your account record and associated data stored in our database, such as your profile information and in-app activity (for example: matches, messages, swipes, reports, and blocks).

Data That May Be Retained

Some data may remain for a limited time or for compliance reasons:

  • Photos you uploaded may remain in storage unless removed before account deletion or removed by request.
  • Backup copies may be retained temporarily by our backend provider (Supabase) as part of automated backups, subject to their retention policies.
  • Purchase and subscription records may be retained by our payment and subscription providers (such as Google Play / Apple App Store and RevenueCat) for financial, legal, and compliance purposes.

Request Full Removal (Including Photos)

If you want us to remove remaining stored photos or any residual data connected to your account, email us at mttotango@hotmail.com. We will process requests within a reasonable timeframe.
